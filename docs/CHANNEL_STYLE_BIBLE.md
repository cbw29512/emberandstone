# Ember & Stone Channel Style Bible

## Purpose

This file is the permanent visual canon for Ember & Stone.

Ember & Stone is not a random dark fantasy image generator. It is a long-running dark fantasy lore channel with a consistent branded visual identity. Every image for every video should feel like it belongs to the same world, even when the story, location, creature, ruin, or mystery changes.

The story changes. The Ember & Stone visual identity does not.

## Channel-Wide Visual Identity

Ember & Stone visuals should feel:

- dark fantasy
- cinematic
- mythic
- ancient
- eerie
- solemn
- haunted
- mysterious
- melancholic
- story-rich
- production-grade

The image style should be a refined dark fantasy illustration style with strong composition, clear readable shapes, atmospheric lighting, and enough detail to feel premium without becoming cluttered or noisy.

## Channel Palette

The normal Ember & Stone palette should stay close to:

- charcoal
- ash gray
- pale stone
- weathered gray
- fog blue
- moonlit blue-gray
- parchment tan
- old wood brown
- deep umber
- restrained amber candlelight
- occasional cold green or faint mineral glow when story-appropriate

The palette should not drift into bright saturated fantasy, neon, cheerful colors, candy colors, or modern cinematic sci-fi colors.

## Lighting Language

Preferred lighting:

- soft atmospheric diffusion
- candlelight accents
- low moonlight
- fog-filtered daylight
- window light
- lantern glow
- dim hearth glow
- narrow shafts of pale light
- restrained rim light for silhouettes

Avoid:

- bright studio lighting
- glossy superhero lighting
- cheerful daylight
- neon lighting
- sci-fi glow unless the story specifically requires an unnatural magical glow

## Mood

The permanent emotional tone is:

- serious
- ancient
- ominous
- quiet
- mythic
- sorrowful
- strange
- mysterious
- suspenseful
- never goofy
- never bright heroic fantasy

The viewer should feel like they are being shown a recovered fragment from a forgotten world.

## Environment Language

Common Ember & Stone environments:

- old stone rooms
- ruined halls

- misty valleys
- mountain villages
- cave mouths
- underground chambers
- ancient bridges
- worn shrines
- forgotten libraries
- empty roads
- dead towns
- ritual spaces
- weathered wood interiors
- parchment-covered desks
- black water
- fog-heavy landscapes

Environments should feel lived-in, old, weathered, and story-rich.

## Character Language

Common character types:

- cloaked travelers
- scholars
- pilgrims
- villagers
- priests

- ageless figures
- doomed witnesses
- quiet cultists
- old chroniclers
- messengers
- watchful strangers

Characters should feel grounded in a dark medieval-fantasy world. Avoid superhero poses, glossy armor glamour, modern fashion, anime styling, and exaggerated cartoon expression.

## Channel Drift Is Forbidden

Do not drift into:

- modern objects
- modern clothing
- sci-fi
- neon
- bright cheerful fantasy
- playful cartoon style
- anime style

- inconsistent rendering quality
- random style switching

- copyrighted character lookalikes

## Image Approval Rule

An image passes only if both are true:

1. It clearly communicates the intended script beat.
2. It visually belongs to Ember & Stone.

An image does not need to match every word literally. It must preserve the important story elements, fit the dark fantasy lore tone, look production-grade, and remain visually consistent with the channel.

## Hard Fail Conditions

Reject an image if:

- it is the wrong scene
- it misses the main story beat
- it misses required key elements
- it looks weak or generic
- it breaks the Ember & Stone visual style
- it has modern objects or modern clothing
- it contains readable text, fake letters, logos, signatures, stamps, or watermarks
- it looks like a different channel
- it has distracting anatomy or composition problems
- it feels cartoonish, goofy, bright, sci-fi, anime, or superheroic
- it uses official copyrighted fantasy art style or recognizable protected characters

## Acceptable Flexibility

Do not reject only because of small literal differences if the scene still works.

Acceptable variations include:

- paper on floor instead of directly on floor
- different camera angle
- symbolic interpretation instead of exact staging
- slight prop placement changes
- scene shown through aftermath instead of direct action
- minor character pose differences
- background simplified to improve focal clarity

The standard is story clarity, channel consistency, and production quality.

## Required Prompt Stack

Every future image prompt should be built from:

1. Channel Consistency Lock
2. Episode Continuity Lock
3. Scene Beat Lock
4. Negative Drift Guard
5. Human Review Gate

## Channel Consistency Lock

This image must match the permanent visual identity of the Ember & Stone channel.

Ember & Stone is a dark fantasy lore channel with a consistent, production-grade visual style. Every image must feel like it belongs to the same branded universe across the entire channel, not just this one video.

Required channel-wide style rules:

- dark fantasy lore illustration
- cinematic, moody, mythic, ancient, eerie, solemn atmosphere
- polished and production-grade finish
- refined stylized illustration with clear readable shapes and strong composition
- muted color palette built around charcoal, ash, pale stone, fog blue, weathered gray, parchment tones, and restrained amber candlelight accents
- soft atmospheric lighting, fog diffusion, moonlight, window light, lantern light, and candlelight
- haunting, mysterious, melancholic tone
- environments should feel ancient, worn, forgotten, and story-rich
- recurring fantasy elements may include ruined interiors, caves, valleys, villages, shrines, books, candles, maps, symbols, ritual objects, and weathered architecture
- characters should fit a grounded dark fantasy world: cloaked travelers, scholars, pilgrims, villagers, children, and mythic figures
- maintain stylistic continuity with the rest of the channel
- prioritize clarity, beauty, narrative relevance, and visual cohesion

Channel drift is forbidden:

- no modern objects
- no modern fashion
- no sci-fi
- no bright high-saturation fantasy
- no playful cartoon style
- no random style switching
- no inconsistent rendering quality
- no weak generic filler images
- no text, letters, logos, stamps, signatures, or watermarks
- no official D&D art style
- no copyrighted character lookalikes

This image must look amazing, cinematic, emotionally resonant, and clearly belong to the Ember & Stone channel.

## Long-Running Channel Rule

This style bible is permanent until intentionally revised. The channel is expected to run indefinitely, so visual decisions should support repeatable production, long-term consistency, and future automation.
